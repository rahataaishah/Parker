Case Study: Buying a Gas Station



The Problem


Peter, an ambitious entrepreneur based in California, had established his gas station business and successfully served customers for several years. Eager to explore new business opportunities, he conducted extensive research and determined that expanding his portfolio by owning an additional gas station would be a lucrative venture.

However, purchasing another gas station required a substantial amount of capital. Peter began exploring financing options to cover the acquisition cost. Initially, he approached traditional lenders, but they expressed hesitation in financing a gas station due to the perceived risks associated with the industry.

Not one to be discouraged, Peter continued his quest for alternative financing solutions and stumbled upon Parker Financial Services Loans. Recognizing their expertise in providing specialized funding for such ventures, he realized that partnering with them would be an ideal fit for his needs. Their loan products, tailored specifically for business expansions like his, offered the necessary funds to acquire the California gas station, coupled with attractive terms, including a low, fixed interest rate and favorable repayment schedules.




The Solution


Peter approached Parker Financial Services Loans, presenting his well-crafted business plan, financial projections, and other requisite documentation. Impressed by his meticulous preparations and unwavering commitment to the project, Parker Financial Services Loans swiftly approved a loan of $1.5 million through their specialized financing program, tailored to facilitate the purchase of the gas station.

With the support of Parker Financial Services Loans, Peter successfully acquired the additional gas station in California and commenced operations. The new location rapidly garnered a loyal customer base due to its competitive pricing and exceptional customer service, reflecting Peter's dedication and attention to detail. This expansion marked a promising chapter in Peter's entrepreneurial journey, inspiring him to contemplate further growth and expansion in the future.

Case Study: Refinancing a Dealership



The Problem


Shawn was the proud owner of a well-established car dealership located in California. Over the years, the dealership had thrived and earned a reputation as a trusted source for both new and used vehicles in the community.

However, Shawn faced a significant challenge: North Star Autos had accumulated a substantial debt of $3.2 million. The high interest rates on the loans were placing considerable financial strain on the business, prompting Shawn to seek a better solution to manage the debt and secure the dealership's future.

After conducting thorough research on various financing options, Shawn determined that securing a loan from Parker Financial Services would be the ideal choice for his situation. Parker Financial Services specialized in providing tailored loans to entrepreneurs like Shawn, offering attractive interest rates and extended repayment terms. This made them the perfect fit for helping him refinance the dealership's existing debt and alleviate the financial burden.




The Solution


With determination, Shawn diligently gathered all the necessary documentation for his loan application with Parker Financial Services. He prepared detailed financial statements, providing a comprehensive overview of North Star Autos' performance, along with a solid plan for managing the business moving forward.

Parker Financial Services carefully evaluated Shawn's application and recognized the immense potential in North Star Autos. They understood that refinancing the dealership's debt would unlock valuable cash flow, enabling Shawn to reinvest in the continuous growth and success of the business. Impressed by his strong application and clear vision for the future, Parker Financial Services approved Shawn's loan request.

Through the refinancing process, Shawn successfully consolidated North Star Autos' debt into a single loan with a lower interest rate, resulting in manageable monthly payments. This newfound financial stability provided the dealership with the necessary resources to continue serving the community and expanding its customer base.

Thanks to the support and guidance of Parker Financial Services and their specialized loan program, Shawn successfully refinanced the dealership's debt, securing a brighter future. The revitalized financial position allowed Shawn to focus on further growth and continue providing exceptional service to the community in California.

Case Study: Buying a Building for a Body Shop



The Problem


Business was flourishing for Jose and his auto body shop in California, but the absence of a proper parking lot was hindering his ability to keep up with the rising demand. Half of the 5,000 sq. ft shop he rented was being utilized for car storage, while the other half was dedicated to repairs. Recognizing the need to expand, Jose began searching for a larger building.

He came across a property for sale that offered a spacious 6,000 sq. ft workspace along with 5,000 sq. ft of parking space, equivalent to around 20 parking spots. In addition to providing ample room for his operations, the mortgage payment for this property would be only half of what he was paying in rent. The listed price for the property was $350,000, making it an appealing prospect for Jose.

Initially, Jose approached a loan officer at another SBA lender for a commercial real estate loan. However, he faced a hurdle when he discovered that the lender's minimum loan amount for commercial real estate was $650,000. Given that this would be his first business loan, Jose was hesitant about taking on such a substantial debt.

Fortunately, the loan officer at the competing loan provider set aside any rivalry and recommended Jose to their institution. Once Jose contacted his loan officer at Parker Financial Services, everything started falling into place. Within just 60 days of initiating the loan application, Jose found himself the proud owner of his new building.




The Solution


Parker Financial Services was able to provide 90% financing, taking into account Jose's strong industry experience and the fact that the loan payment would be lower than his previous rent. With the move to the new building, Jose wasted no time in hiring two additional technicians to tackle the backlog of jobs that had accumulated over the past month.

Moreover, the lower mortgage payment resulting from the loan proved to be a game-changer for Jose's business during the unforeseen arrival of the COVID-19 pandemic in March 2020. While three out of five competing auto body shops in the area closed due to the pandemic, Jose's business thrived with reduced competition and the ability to maintain "business as usual."

Thanks to the support and financial relief provided by Parker Financial Services, Jose's auto body shop continues to prosper. The expanded facilities, increased staff, and affordable financing have played crucial roles in driving the success of his business.
